Notes for Xainte Dupont
Xaintes DUPONT was the widow of Michael LERMUSIER and may have been related to (or the daughter of?) Michel Dupont, buried 4-6-1608, who was of the same parish. With Perrine N... he had three known children, Renee, b. 10-2-1603, a child buried 5-1-1603, and a girl, buried 27-8-1613. This is according to Archange Godbout in FCAGR, v. 3, #4, p. 226
