Sally Davidson March 1, 1949 - August 11, 2017 Peacefully, at Westmount, Quebec on Friday, August 11, 2017 at the age of 68

Beloved sister of Pam and Andy (Janet). Cherished aunt of James, Jonathan (Molly), Katie & Charlie; Carrie (Michael) & Russell (Jessica).

Much-loved great-aunt of Kaitlyn and Darian; Noah & Lachlan;

William, Alistair & Cameron.

Lovingly remembered by cousins Andrea & Peter; Joan & Jim and sister-in-law Janice. Fondly remembered by her close friend Al Stavro, and many other friends and business associates. Predeceased by her brother Peers in 2001.

Funeral arrangements entrusted to Mount Royal Funeral Complex, 1297 Chemin de la Foret, Outremont, Quebec H2V 2P9.

A Memorial Service to celebrate Sally's life will be held in the chapel at the above location at 11:00am on Friday, September 1, followed by reception. Private family burial. Condolences may be left online at mountroyalcem.com.

In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to ALS Society of Canada.
