Notes for Shirley Davidson
[SD] Another family history story - my great-uncle,
Shirley Davidson (yes, uncle) drowned in 1907(?) with William
Hingston's
daughter [Aileen --CRP]. They didn't find the bodies for several
days, and it was all
over the front pages of both English papers as the search went
on..what
with one being the daughter of Sir William and the other being the
son of
Sir Charles!! Later, in my research, I was reading the bio of Sir
Charles in "Les Juges de Québec", and there was a note handwritten in
the
book following his bio, which loosely translated said that they
committed
suicide because they couldn't get married! I checked with the
archivist
at ANQ, and she said the person who made those notes was a researcher
at
the courthouse (now long retired). I guess that would be something
the
families would try and keep quiet.
