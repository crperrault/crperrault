Notes for Rev. Samuel Hales
[King] Noted preacher and curate of Cork Cathedral.

The name "Hales" is anglicized form of Healy, Hayles, and MacHale.

http://wc.rootsweb.ancestry.com/cgi-bin/igm.cgi?op...haronmh&id=I2330
Samuel entered Trinity College Dublin at the age of 18 in 1726. He became the rector of Kilbrittain Parish, County Cork 21 May 1752. His will was probated in 1782.
