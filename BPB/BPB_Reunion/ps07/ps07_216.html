Notes for Jean Godefroy De Lintot
[DBC] not "Jean Baptiste", as he has usually been called by historians on the basis of the 1666 Census; "Lintot", from the name of a small municipality in the district of Caux, not "Linctot". Interpreter, seigneur, member of the Communaute des Habitants. Relative of Jean Paul Godefroy, brother of Thomas Godefroy. Head of the Godefroy de Tonnancour family. Letters of nobility were granted in 1668 but, because of an administrative error, never registered within the required time, and were theoretically cancelled. They were finally recognized by the King in 1718. He was never wealthy, in spite of various recommendations from Frontenac to the King for support.
