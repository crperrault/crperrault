Notes for George Beasley
1748 [marriage] on 13 Aug 1843 at church of Westerham, Kent, George Bezley, of full age, bachelor, labourer, of Westerham, son of Joseph Bezley, labourer, married Jane Tidy, 20, spinster, labourer, of Westerham, dau of Michael Tidy, labourer. With the mark of the spouses, witnesses James TIdy and Letitia Tidy.

James (25, b ca 1813) and Letitia (30 b ca 1811) Tidy appear in the 1841 census of Westerham, where the household also includes George Richardson (9) and Robert Richardson (7) an Sarah (1) Richardson. It appears as if Letitia had previously been married. James could have been Jane’s older brother. This would also indicate that Jane’s parents were no longer alive.
