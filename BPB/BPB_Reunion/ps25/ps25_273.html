James Foxlee was born in Ayot (or Ayott) St. Peter, just outside Welwyn, Herts, about 1820. In 1841, he was still with his parents at Ayott St Peter. Around 1845, he was married to Ann, who was born in 1828 in Stapleford Abbotts, Essex. Their daughter, Mary Ann, was born in 1846 in Stapleford Abbotts. Their son WIlliam F was born in 1851 in Datchworth, Herts, where they were for the 1851 census. Around 1852, their son Richard Augustus was born in Stanford-le-Hope, Essex, 23 miles from Stapleford Abbotts. They are not found in 1861. In 1871, they are in Harrow, MSX, and by 1881, 5 km away in Ruislip,
