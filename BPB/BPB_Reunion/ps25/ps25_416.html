158 The Orillia Packet, 23 July, 1880
Died -- At the residence of her son, George Baye, South Orillia, Ont the 20th of July, Julia, relict of the late Antoine Gaudaur, aged 82
Julia Sanscoucie was previously the wife of Honore Philippe /Bailly/Baye/Bailey who died in Mara Township Oct 12, 1829. Julia died July 20, 1880, 3rd Concession, South Orillia, Simcoe County.
Julia was married abt 1863 to Antoine Gaudaur and probably lived with him for sometime at Fawn Bay, Mara Township before leaving, to live out her life with her son George in Orillia.

My records show Honore and Julia had at least 5 children.
Alexander, Francis, George (1814-1904), Pauline , Joseph (1829-1906). Honore and Antoine more than likely knew each other from the war of 1812 as both were posted at Drummond Island and then came to raise their families in Simcoe County.
I am a descendant of Antoine Gaudaur - Mary Shilling. Mary died Nov 27, 1848 in Orillia.
