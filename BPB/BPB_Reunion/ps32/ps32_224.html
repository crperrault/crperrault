Notes for Dr Beshara Manasseh
[Tony Manasseh personal communication] Dr. Beshara had graduated from AUB 1st classes of medical doctors in 1877 and met Theophilos Waldmeier who was a missionary who had fled Abysynia to Levant (Lebanon). Th. Waldmeier was meeting Cybil & Eli Jones from MAine who had come to establish Quaker presence in the rush of missionaries in the 1870s. That's when Dr. Beshara was convinced of the Quaker way and became a Friend, So Beshara took his brothers Antonios and Spiridon to Brummana where he founded the first Hospital and Antonios and Spiridon went thru high school.

327 shows arrival at Liverpool on Camania on 1909-8-22 from New York of Beshara E Manaseh merchant age 27 b Syria and Doris (Dored?) Manasseh 21 b Syria, final destination Beirut
