Notes for George Mair Benington

Still unmarried in 1901, living with parents

337 [p 45) Printer Educated at james Wood’s school at Alderley Edge (later moved to Colwyn Bay) and Ackworth. Apprenticed in Nottingham and after working in London went to Liverpool in 1893 becoming a director of E Benington & Son, printers. He consisently declined orders for work which conflicted with his principles. He was active in local Quaker work but declined invitations to serve on the City Council or serve as a Poor Law guardian. He died at his home, 59 Newsham Dr, Liverpool 21 jan 1933. He was unm. The Friend (1933) sci 136, Ackworth Old Scholars Assoc Report (1933) lii 82.
