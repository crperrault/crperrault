I have an Anthony William WILSON, born at HighWray 9th May 1845; married
at Colthouse, near Ambleside, 2nd Sept, 1875, Sarah Maria, eldest daughter
of late Wilson ARMISTEAD, of Leeds.

Also: Hannah WILSON, born at High Wray 21st June, 1792, died at Leeds 21st
March 1827, having married 15th April, 1818, to Joseph, son of Joseph and
Elizabeth Armistead. He was born at Leeds 1793,Aug 22, and died there 3rd
Oct, 1861, having had 4 sons and 1 daugfhter.:
(This Hannah would be the aunt of the above Anthony WILSON).

Hannah and Mary WILSON were sisters of my GGGrandfather, George WILSON who

Edward, I believe , was George WILSON'S nephew.
Edwards children were Hannah and Joseph(named after Edwards parents)
