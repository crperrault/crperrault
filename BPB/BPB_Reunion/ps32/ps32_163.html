1964 Benington, Harold. Certified Public Accountant of 140 S Dearborn St, was born Dec 9 1875 in England. He is a partner in the firm Ernest Reckitt and Company; and President of the Illinois Society of Certified Public Accountants.

1961 Benington Harold. b Stockton on Tees, Eng Dec 19 1875; educ St Peters School York and Bedford Grammar School. Five yrs under articles to HF Knight & CO London; two years as senior acct Monkhouse, Stoneham & Co London; two yrs, comptroller SHsopps Ltd brewers; 13 yrs practice as acct in US AA England; CPA Illinois and Pennsylvania. Partner Ernest Reckitt & Co . FOrmer Pres Ill Soc CPAs and mem council inst of Pub Accts. Served 20 mos in Army, entering with rank of Capt and holding rank of Lieut Col at time of discharge. Was in charge finance branch of Dept of Military Aeronautics. Holds commission as Liet Col Signal Reserve Corpt. Mem AIA Chicago Athletic Assn and Glen Vier Club. M. Lucile Orchard Mar 1 1920. Home 922 Glengyle Pl. Office 708 Marquette Bldg Chicago.
