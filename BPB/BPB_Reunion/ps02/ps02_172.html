Notes for William Frederick Benington
Lived until about 17 in Brooklyn, then for several years in various
cities of the US, but mostly around Boston. Electrical engineer,
without a high-school diploma. Worked for Stone and Webster, global
construction company. Invented a system that would deelectrify a
third rail if approached by an individual.

After his death, Marjorie lived with HHB and Lucette; Frederick
lived with George and Agnes.
