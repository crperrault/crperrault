[NYT November 28, 1982 ]

ARTHUR BENNINGTON


Published: November 28, 1982

Arthur Bennington, a partner since 1977 in the Wall Street brokerage firm of Alex Brown & Sons, died yesterday at the Norwalk (Conn.) Hospital.

He was 53 years old and had suffered a heart attack Friday night at his home in New Canaan, Conn.

Mr. Bennington had previously been an exchange floor partner in Robert W. Baird & Company and in Drexel Harriman Ripley, which no longer exists under that name. He had also served as a vice president of the State National Bank of Connecticut.

Mr. Bennington is survived by his wife, the former Mathilde Johnston; a daughter, Mathilde, and a son, George A., both of New Canaan, and a sister, Edwina Evers of San Francisco.
