Notes for Josette Laframboise
280 [Frank Beaubien] Her mother was a half-breed Indian of the Pottawatomi. She lived with the Kinzie family before and at the time of the Fort Dearborn Massacre. She made her escape with the Kinzies. She was a second cousin to Joseph laframboise who was living at mackinac and whose daughter, also named Josette, married Lt. Col Benjamn Pierce, a brother of Pres. Franklin Pierce, when he was in command of Fort mackinac about 1815-1816. Josette and Jean Baptiste had 12 children.
