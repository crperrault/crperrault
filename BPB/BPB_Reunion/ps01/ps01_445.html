Notes for Jacques De Gaspé Beaubien CBE
[Who's WHo] B.Sc (McGill 1906), DSc (Manitoba); Consulting engineer
and businessman. Pres, Eng Institute of Canada.

[Repertoire 1996-7 des Ingenieurs conseils du Canada] Taught at
McGill 1906-8; Westinghouse (Pittsburgh) 1908; consulting
engineering practice 1908 joined by James Leonard Busfield 1922-27 as
Beaubien, Busfield & Co. Beaubien Ltd in 1927; Founding member of
Corp. of Prof Engineers of Québec 1920; First Pres of Assn of
Consulting Engineers of Canada 1925-1949; Electrical Comm of City of
Montréal 1912-57; Natl Chm of Canadian War Savings Committee during
WWII; Member of NRC; Director of Westburne Oil and other companies.
CBE 1944. Pres of Eng Inst of Canada 1944.

The ACEC created in 1984 the Beaubien Award in his honour.
