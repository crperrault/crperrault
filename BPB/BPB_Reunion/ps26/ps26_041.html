Notes for Thomas Foxlee
[confirmed]
THomas Foxlee and Mary had 9 ch in Welwyn and then in Ayott St Peter 1820-1836, of which James, b 1820, was the eldest; one child was named Richard Thompson. IN the 1851 C, Thomas is shown as b ca 1779 in Welwyn, and in the 1841 in Herts. In 1817, at St Andrew Holborn in London, where he was living and as a widower, he m Mary Thompson, a spinster from Welvingin Herts (which I cannot find). His first wife may have been Frances Benton, whom he m at St Martin in the Fields in 1803 (no parents given) with whom there are 4 ch b 1804-1808, in London, then in Welwyn. The only birth consistent with ca 1779, Welwyn is ch 31 Oct 1779 Welwyn, s/o Joseph Foxlee & Sarah Rowley (which can be found at familysearch w both parents named, but not at Ancestry). He died in 1854 at Hatfield, Herts. No probate found.
