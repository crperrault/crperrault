Called to the Bar in June 1872 and started practicing in Madras

HM’s affidavit, filed 9 Jul 1874:
Resides at 28 Richmond Crescent, RIchmond Rd, Islington MSX, wife of Richard Cleghorn Miller late of Berhampore, in the presidency of Madras in East Indies, but now of London, Esq, barrister. Married 4 Jul 1865 as Helen White, spinster, at St Peter, Newcastle upon Tyne, Norts. Then lives at Eccleston Terr, Eccleston Sq, Belgravia and at other places in England and afterwards at Madras and Berhampore until 4 Sep 1873 when she separated. Four children, one day, 3 sons ages 8 [b 1866], 6 [1868], 3[1871], and 6 weeks[1874]. In Islington in 1870. Called to the Bar in June 1872 and started practicing in Madras. n Madras Jul 1872-May 1873 residing with paternal uncle John Miller. Left Berhampore 26 Sep 1873 for Madras.
