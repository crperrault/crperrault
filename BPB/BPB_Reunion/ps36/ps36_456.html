2150 Elizabeth Leavens (d 1665). Preacher in Westmorland in northern England, who joined with George Fox in 1652. She was attacked and badly neaten in Oxford in 1652. Leavens later married Thomas Holmes, a Quaker minister who also joined with Fox in 1652. They lived and preached in Wales.
