To be buried in Kendal Chuch.
To four poor people of Kendal 5 Sh . at the descreation of my overseers.

To my eldest son Everard Gilpin, living in London, 20 Sh . within a year.

To my daughter Ruth Gilpin _my little bible _ .

To my syster Elisabeth Edmonson, one roofe band, one smock, one white petticoate, one black cloth safeguard; on condition she pay Edward Bateman for a stone of lime for which I have passed my word for paiement.

My friends and cousins, Allan Gilpin and Lawrence Bolton, Supervisors.
