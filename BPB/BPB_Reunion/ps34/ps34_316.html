At Wrockwardine co Salop, aged 73, The Rev Jospha Gilpin MA who of r45 years exercised the ministerial functions in that parish … was an intimate friend of the celebrated John Fletcher, who presided over the adjacent parish of Madely, and was presented to the Pastoral charge, from which the hand of death has now separated him, by the late Earl of Shrewsbury, in consequence of a petition sent to that nobleman by the Society of Friends, so greatly was the excellence of hs character estimated by that discerning body of Christians, who form no mean portion of the population in his vicinity…author of “A monument of Parental Affection to a dear and only son”, two volumnes of sermons, a translation from the French of “Fletcher’s Portrait of St Paul, or Model for Christian Pastors”, an edition of “Madeleine’s Alarm” and a reprint of “Bunyan’s Pilgrim;s Progress” in more correct language than the original.
