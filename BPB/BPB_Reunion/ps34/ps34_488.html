2109 [mar] on Thursday [jul 2 1840], at the Friends Meeting House, Stockton on Tees, Mr Robert Thorpe, of Sheffield, draper, to Miss Smith, daughter of Mr George Smith, of the former place, wholesale grocer. A flag was hoisted on the warehouse of the bride’s father, all the vessels in the harbour had their colors flying, and the “Battle House” (in which Mr Smith is a large shareholder) was gaily decorated on the occasion.

The only marriage of a Robert Thorp[e] to a Smith recorded in Civ Reg in 1840 is Robert m Ann Smith, recorded Jun 1840 Darlington.
