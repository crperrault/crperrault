Notes for Sarah YARWOOD
1978 Note about Sarah in George Benington’s record:
"Sarah, the mother, died at Walton, age 37, 29.4.1837. She was a woman of retired habits,studiously cultivating the tender minds of her infant children, and improving upon them the important truths contained in the Scriptures. She was indeed an anxious parent and a truly devoted and affectionate wife".

No information as to why this George is in London. Houndsditch is very much 'city of London' and is now in a conservation area. One may speculate about his business in London, how he met Sarah why he is then back in Yorkshire when his children are born. Sarah is a Yorkshire girl.

Walton is near Wakefield.
