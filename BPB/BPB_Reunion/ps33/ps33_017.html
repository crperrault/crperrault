Motor racing Baronet Sir Gawaine Baillie, 7th Baronet, of Warninglid, Sussex, died in hospital, 21 December, 2003. He was aged 69. Married with one son and one daughter. The title is now inherited by his son, born in 1973. Sir Gawaine was a grandson maternally of the 1st (and last) Baron Queenborough, GBE (1861-1949), a great-grandson of General Lord Alfred Paget, CB (1816-88) and a great-great-grandson of the 1st Marquess of Anglesey, KG, GCB, GCH, KMT, KSG (1768-1854) by his second wife, Lady Charlotte, dau of the 1st Earl Cadogan (1728-1807).
