Notes for Luce Perrault
Married at 15 and had 11 children. Her husband describes her as "the gentlest, the most charitable, the least pretentious" of women. Noted philanthropist: Association des Dames de la Charité, Montréal Asylum for Aged and Infirm Women, Asile de la Providence. Survived her husband by 56 years.
