Notes for Sen. Lawrence Alexander Wilson
Son of Scottish Canadian father and FC mother. COllege Ste. Marie. Went into wholesale wine and spirits business. Noted for philanthropies. Liberal MP for Vaudreuil Soulanges 1925-30. Senator 1930. Knight commander of ordre of St Gregoire le Grand 1933.

Acquired the de Beaujeu manor at Coteau-du Lac in 1914.
