John Robinson Peverley
B. Aug.2,1814, London, England
D. July 26, 1887, Rivere du Loup, Que.

Elizabeth Armstrong
B. Dec. 17, 1820 Quebec City, Que.

Ann Peverley
B. 1802, London, England
D. Oct. 10, 1882 Montreal, Que.
M. Oct. 26, 1826, Limehouse, London, England ........ to..... Henry Gear (no info)


John Robinson Peverley's parents:

John Peverley
B. Bollingrobe, County Mays, Province of Connaught, Ireland
D. at 54 years of age. Believed to be buried in Durham, England.
His father was a sea captain in the Greenland Trade. He had 2 brothers, Robinson and William.
M. 1801, London England ........ to.........
Charlotte Veale
B. 1781, Sunderland, Durham County, England. An only daughter, had several brothers. One, Johnston Veale, was a sea captain and boat owner. The other brothers settled in the USA.
D. Aug. 27, 1849, Sillery Cove, Que. at the residence of her daughter Mrs. Henry Gear, nee Ann Peverley.

I hope this helps.

John Robinson Peverley was my great great great grandfather. My great great grandfather was Clavering T.S.A. Peverley, the brother of your George Veale Peverley. I curious to hear where you fit into the mix. I have a lot of other information as well, but nothing further down from George and Charlotte on that branch. I'd be happy to trade whatever info you have with copies of what I have.
