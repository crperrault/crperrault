Notes for Alexander Ogilvie (4)
[Sellars] Storekeeper. Operated an ashery and was involved in
lumbering with Alexander McBain (m. Susan Davidson). Is said to have
averaged 250 barrels each season, and pearled 50 barrels. They were
involved in lumbering for masts and oars. He organised a school in
1835. The farmers agreed to apy at the rate of a bushel of corn per
scholar for a winter's schooling of 155 days, the corn being valued
at 70c a bushel.

[C1861 Huntingdon] in St Anicet Alex Ogilvie, 67 m Agnes, 65, w/ 3
kids Alex 27, Norman 23, Susan 19. He lived on Conc 2 Lot 20, 100
acres.
